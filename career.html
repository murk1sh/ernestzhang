Career & Education Goals

Preferred Engineering Discipline

My preferred engineering discipline is Computer Science and Machine Learning. Computer scientists study and solve complex problems in computing, developing new technologies, software, and algorithms to improve how people interact with computers and systems, and often work in various industries like business, science, and medicine. Typically, they deeply understand advanced mathematics, programming languages, and even physics.

This field resonates with me because it is the future.

Dream Job

My dream job is to become a Machine Learning Engineer at a leading technology company or research institution. This involves designing and deploying machine learning models to solve complex real-world problems. For example, to solve the lead pipe poisioning happening in Flint, Michigan, data scientists trained a model to predict where to excavate for potential lead pipes. I hope to continue this but for other problems.

My passion for machine learning started when I got defeated by a Chess.com bot. I wanted to learn how Stockfish evaluated the score.
